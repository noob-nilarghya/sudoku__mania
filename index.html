RULE

As you can clearly see that the 9x9 sudoku board can be divided into nine 3x3 region called blocks. Fill in the numbers 1-9 exactly once in every row, column, and 3x3 region (also called blocks).

Abiding the above condition, fill each of the unfilled grid. Once all the grids are filled, click on "Check" to verify your solution.

Get stuck? Click on "Reveal" to see the solution.
